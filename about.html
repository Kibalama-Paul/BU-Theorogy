Our Story

Bugema University is a private, co-educational Ugandan university affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist Church. It is a part of the Seventh-day Adventist education system, the world's second largest Christian school system.

Our university offers worship services and spaces that cater to diverse religious and spiritual needs. Students can engage in prayer, meditation, and other spiritual activities to promote a sense of community, mindfulness, and well-being.

Philosophical Theory

Philosophical Theology: This field explores the relationship between faith and reason. Philosophical theologians use philosophical methods to analyze religious concepts and doctrines.

Liberation Theology

This field emphasizes the importance of social justice and seeks to use theology to liberate the oppressed. Liberation theology is often associated with Christianity in Latin America.

Feminist

This field challenges traditional theological interpretations that have been seen as patriarchal and explores the experiences of women in light of religious traditions.

Systematic Theory

This field attempts to create a unified and rational understanding of religious doctrines. Systematic theologians often draw on philosophy and reason to analyze and explain their faith.
